About me

I am currently an MSc student in Mathematics and Computing at Indian Institute of Technology (Indian School of Mines), Dhanbad (IIT ISM Dhanbad), and a student of Cosine Lab of IIT ISM Dhanbad.

I did my bachelor's in Mathematics(Hons.) at Kirori Mal College, University of Delhi, India in 2024. During my study, I finished a small project on DYNAMICS OF THE LORENZ CURVE under the supervision of Mr. Vishal Dhawan, Assistant Professor Department of Mathematics.

I have been getting teaching training graduating at Hun Sen Regional Pedagogy Center, Kandal in 2020 to be a civil servant under Ministry of Education, Youth and Sport. In 2021, I was a mathematics teacher at Samdach Akak Moha Thoma Pouthisal Chea Sim Prek Anhchan High School (New Generation).

In 2018, I completed high school diploma at Bun Rany Hun Sen High School, R'lea Bier.
